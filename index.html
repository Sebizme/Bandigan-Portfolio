ABOUT
ME

Designer · Creator · Data Analysis

Hello! I'm Sean Bandigan. People often spell or pronounce my name in different ways—Siyan, Shaun, Seen—but it's simply Sean. I'm a 20-year-old Information Technology student at Ateneo de Davao University. While many IT students focus heavily on programming and technical development, my interests take a different direction. My passion has always been rooted in multimedia, creativity, and visual expression. Even though I chose Information Technology as my degree, I've always been drawn to the world of design—especially UI/UX, digital media, and visual storytelling.

Instead of pursuing a formal degree in multimedia, I decided to take a more flexible path, building my creative skills independently alongside my IT studies. Through personal projects, self-learning, and continuous exploration, I've been developing a skill set that blends both technology and creativity. I enjoy creating digital experiences that are not only functional but also meaningful and visually engaging. For me, it's not just about writing code—it's about using technology as a tool to design, communicate, and bring ideas to life.
